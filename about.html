About Fund Vision Uganda

Fund Vision Uganda is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting quality education for all children and youth in Uganda. We believe that education is the key to unlocking individual and societal potential.

Through our innovative Grant Management and Monitoring System, we aim to:

  • Empower local organizations working in education to access the resources they need
  • Increase transparency and accountability in grant management
  • Facilitate collaboration between education stakeholders
  • Track the impact of education interventions and ensure sustainable change

Our Vision

Fund Vision Uganda envisions a future where all Ugandan children have access to quality education, regardless of their background or socioeconomic status.

Our Mission

Our mission is to build a stronger education system in Uganda by providing efficient grant management tools, fostering partnerships, and advocating for educational equity.

Our Team

Fund Vision Uganda is led by a team of passionate professionals with extensive experience in education, grant management, and technology. We are committed to serving the needs of our stakeholders and making a positive impact on the lives of Ugandan children.
