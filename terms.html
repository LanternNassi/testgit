Eligibility

  • Eligible applicants include registered non-profit organizations, educational institutions, and civil society organizations working towards achieving SDG 4 targets in Uganda.
  • Organizations must demonstrate a strong track record, financial stability, and commitment to ethical practices.

Grant Application Process

  • All applications must be submitted electronically through the System.
  • Applicants are responsible for ensuring the accuracy and completeness of their application information.
  • Fund Vision Uganda reserves the right to request additional information or clarifications.

Grant Selection and Award

  • Applications are evaluated by a panel of experts based on alignment with SDG 4 targets, project feasibility, and potential impact.
  • The selection process is competitive, and Fund Vision Uganda is not obligated to award any grants.
  • All applicants will be notified of the review decision in a timely manner.

Grant Management and Reporting

  • Successful grantees are required to use grant funds for the purposes outlined in their approved proposal.
  • Grantees must submit regular progress reports through the System, tracking project implementation, budget utilization, and impact on SDG 4 indicators.
  • Fund Vision Uganda reserves the right to conduct monitoring visits and audits to ensure compliance with these Terms and Conditions.
