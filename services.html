Streamlining Your Grant Management for SDG 4 Impact

Fund Vision Uganda offers a comprehensive suite of services designed to simplify the grant management process and empower organizations working towards achieving SDG 4 targets in Uganda.

Pre-Award Services

  • **Eligibility Screening:** We ensure your organization meets the criteria for SDG 4-aligned grant programs.
  • **Proposal Development Support:** We guide you in crafting compelling proposals that align with SDG 4 goals and Fund Vision Uganda's priorities.
  • **Budget Development Assistance:** We help you create realistic and well-defined budgets for your educational intervention.

Award Management Services

  • **Streamlined Application Process:** Apply for grants electronically through our user-friendly online portal.
  • **Efficient Review and Selection:** Our transparent review process ensures fair and objective grant allocation based on SDG 4 targets.
  • **Clear Grant Agreements:** We provide clear and concise grant agreements outlining project expectations and deliverables.

Post-Award Services

  • **Real-Time Progress Tracking:** Monitor project progress and budget utilization through our online reporting tools.
  • **Ongoing Support and Guidance:** We offer ongoing support and guidance throughout the grant implementation process.
  • **Impact Measurement and Reporting:** We help you measure the impact of your project on achieving SDG 4 targets in Uganda.
