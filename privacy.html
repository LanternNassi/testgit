Privacy Policy

Fund Vision Uganda is committed to protecting the privacy of individuals who use our grant management system ("System"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information that you provide through the System.

Information We Collect

  • Registration Information: When you register to use the System, we may collect information such as your name, organization name, email address, and phone number.
  • Grant Application Information: When you submit a grant application, we collect information included in your proposal, such as your organization's mission, project description, budget details, and contact information for key personnel.
  • System Usage Information: We may collect information about how you use the System, such as the pages you visit, the features you access, and the time stamps of your activity.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process your grant application and evaluate your eligibility for funding.
  • To communicate with you regarding your grant application, including sending updates, notifications, and award decisions.
  • To manage and monitor grant projects, including tracking progress reports and ensuring compliance with grant terms.
  • To improve the System and its functionality.
  • To generate reports on grant applications and funding trends (in a way that does not identify individual users).

Information Sharing and Disclosure

  • We will not share your personal information with any third party without your consent, except as required by law or to fulfill our grant management responsibilities.
  • We may share aggregate, non-personally identifiable information with third-party service providers who help us operate the System.
  • We may disclose your information if required by law, such as in response to a court order or subpoena.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no website or internet transmission is completely secure. We cannot guarantee the security of your information.

Your Choices

  • You can access and update your registration information at any time by logging into the System and visiting your profile settings.
  • You can choose to opt out of receiving marketing communications from us.
  • You can request to delete your information from the System by contacting us (provide contact information here).

Children's Privacy

Our System is not intended for children under the age of 3. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 3 years.
